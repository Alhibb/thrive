About Decentralized Harmony

Our project is a community-driven content moderation tool using blockchain for transparency and fairness. We aim to minimize bias, reward good moderation and put the power back into the hands of the users. By decentralizing moderation, we promote a more accountable approach to online platforms.

Key Features

Transparent Moderation

Blockchain ensures every moderation action is visible and auditable.

Community-Led Governance

Users actively participate in shaping content policies through voting and proposals.

Reward System

Users earn tokens for accurately flagging inappropriate content.

Reduced Bias

Decentralized decision-making minimizes centralized control and bias.
